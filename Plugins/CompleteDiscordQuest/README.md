# CompleteDiscordQuest for BetterDiscord

A BetterDiscord(BD) plugin that completes you multiple discord quests in background simultaneously.

## Credits:

This is a porting for BetterDiscord of a [snippet](https://gist.github.com/aamiaa/204cd9d42013ded9faf646fae7f89fbb) made by [aamiaa](https://github.com/aamiaa).

## Features:

- **Accept Quests Automatically**: 
  - Whether to accept available quests automatically.
- **UI Elements**:
  - Whether to show the quests button in the title bar.
  - Whether to show the quests button in the settings bar.
  - Whether to show badges on the quests button.
- **Quest Type Filters**:
  - Whether to farm video quests automatically.
  - Whether to farm desktop games quests automatically.
  - Whether to farm streaming quests automatically.
  - Whether to farm activities quests automatically.
- **Quest Rewards Filters**:
  - Whether to farm reward codes automatically.
  - Whether to farm in-game quests automatically.
  - Whether to farm discord user appearance decorations automatically.
  - Whether to farm orbs automatically.
  - Whether to farm fractional premium automatically.
- **Quest Menu Filter**: The filter to use in the quest menu (Suggested, Most Recent, Expiring Soon, Started).
